Experiment Instructions

Aim of the Experiment

This simulation demonstrates Newton's Second Law of Motion: F = ma

How to Use

  1. You can adjust the mass of the trolley using the slider - keep this unchanged while gathering acceleration data
  2. Apply a force of 0.1 N to the trolley and click "Start Experiment" to begin the simulation
  3. The time taken for the trolley to travel 1 m and the final velocity of the trolley will be recorded
  4. Enter your calculated value of acceleration in the table
  5. Repeat three times and take an average
  6. Increase the force by 0.1 N and repeat until at least 5 different forces have been used
  7. You can gather more data for a trolley of a different mass
